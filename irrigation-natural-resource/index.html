Considerations

Afghanistan's mountainous and arid landscape is highly subject to environmental pressures. Drought, floods, deforestation, overgrazing, landslides and erosion present substantial challenges to Afghanistan's agricultural sector. However, the negative effects of these issues can be reduced through improved natural resource management.
