<h3><a name="pest-management" id="pest-management"></a><a name="pest-management" id="pest-management"></a><strong>Pest Management</strong></h3>
<ul><li>Common pests and diseases in eggplant are flea beetles, <a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/fsinsectaphidsucipm.pdf" class="internal-link" target="_self"><strong>aphids</strong></a>,<strong></strong> bacterial wilt, <a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/Verticillium_Wilt_Tomato_UC_IPM.pdf" class="internal-link" target="_self"><strong>verticillium wilt</strong></a>, <a href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/FS_Disease_Phytophthora_Root_Rot.pdf" class="internal-link" title="Eggplant Disease - Phytophthora Root Rot"><strong>phytophthora blight</strong></a><strong>,</strong> <strong><a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/fs-insect-leafhoppers-uc-ipm.pdf" class="internal-link" target="_self">leafhoppers</a>,<a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/fs-mites-uc-ipm.pdf" class="internal-link" target="_self"> mites</a>, phomopsis blight, <strong class="internal-link" title=""> <a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/fspestweednutsedge.pdf" class="internal-link" target="_self">nutsedge</a></strong>, and <strong><a title="" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/eggplant/fspestweedbermudagrass.docx" class="internal-link" target="_self">bermudagrass</a>. </strong>Plant varieties resistant to pathogens and follow IPM practices.<strong><br /></strong></li>
