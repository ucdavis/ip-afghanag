- '<strong><strong>Primary opportunities </strong></strong>include: long<strong style="text-align: left; "><a class="internal-link" href="/fruits-nuts-vegetables/Vegetables/files/okra/PPT_Crop_rotation_Cover_Cropping_TerAvest.pdf" target="_self" title=""><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>crop rotation</a></strong> and proper irrigation and fertilization to reduce build up and susceptibility of disease and soil pathogens; plant disease-resistant varieties, control for other pests (such as white fly), discourage cultivation of okra around cotton fields, intercrop with non-host crops, and collect and burn diseased okra/ bhindi plants to reduce spread of disease; apply soapy sprays, malathion, or Azadiracthin (aphid control) and carbofuran at the time of sowing (leafhopper control).'
