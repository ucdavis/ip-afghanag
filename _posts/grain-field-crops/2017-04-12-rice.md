  <h2>Pest Management</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Rice has a number of pest problems including small aquatic animals, armyworms, grasshoppers, molds, rots and blight. Crop rotations, pesticides, flooding the fields, and planting resistant varieties can help to reduce these problems.</li>
