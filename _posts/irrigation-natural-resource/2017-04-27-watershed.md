care: "The country’s limited forest resources suffered depletion to meet demands from lucrative export markets and for firewood. Severe overgrazing and the recent drought have contributed to the decline of the livestock population, and reduction in ground cover in hillsides has led to floods, widespread soil erosion, and reduced water retention in aquifers. (from World Bank Report)"
problem-opportunities:
  - "<b>Upper watershed (mountains and non-irrigated plateaus)</b>"
  - "<ul>"
  - "<b>Priority problems</b> include rill and gully (water) erosion, wind erosion, poor grazing practices, root excavation, excessive tree and brush removal."
  - "<b>Priority Opportunities</b> include control grazing, terracing, loose stone check dams (See FAO Watershed Management Manual - Gully Control); improved and increased fodder production in irrigated areas; nursery improvement for range rehabilitation."
  - "</ul>"
  - "<b>Streambank erosion due to flooding</b>"
  - "<ul>"
  - "<b>Priority problems</b> include loss of agricultural land base, destruction of infrastructure (bridges, buildings and dams)"
  - "<b>Priority Opportunities</b> include check dams, gabion baskets (picture at right), retaining walls"
  - "</ul>"
  - "<b>Deforestation</b>"
  - "<ul>"
  - "<b>Priority problems</b> include heavy demand for firewood as primary fuel source and grazing pressure from livestock"
  - "<b>Priority Opportunities</b> include alternative fuel sources (particularly gas and mini-hydropower using local waterways), public awareness, protecting young trees from animals to permit natural regrowth, pistachio forests"
  - "</ul>"
  - "These priorities and opportunities are based on feedback from John Groninger (SIU) and Ahmad Zia Mirzada (MAIL)."
