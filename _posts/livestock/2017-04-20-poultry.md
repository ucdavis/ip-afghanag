problem-opportunities:
  - "<b>Priority problems</b> of poultry production in Afghanistan are disease (especially <a href='../files/poultry-newcastle.pdf'>Newcastle Disease</a>), lack of housing (which means birds are lost to predators and bird health is difficult to monitor) and adequate nutrition."
  - "<b>Priority Opportunities</b> are vaccinate chickens against disease (especially <a href='../files/poultry-newcastle.pdf'>Newcastle Disease</a>), provide housing, improve practices for introducing new birds (isolate for two weeks) and improve nutrition. Note: Once Newcastle Disease is better managed, other diseases such as <a href='../files/poultry-coccidiosis.pdf'>coccidiosis</a>, <a href='../files/poultry-bursal.pdf'>infectious bursal disease</a>, and ectoparasites (such as mites) will become the next major limitations."
